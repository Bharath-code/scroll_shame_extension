# Pricing — ScrollShame

ScrollShame offers a one-time purchase model. No subscriptions.

## Free
- Price: $0
- Features:
  - Weekly Chaos Score
  - Score delta tracking
  - Clean Week Mode
  - 1 roast voice (The Therapist)
  - Streak tracking
  - Image export (with footer)

## Pro
- Price: $12 (One-time payment)
- Features:
  - Everything in Free
  - All 5 roast voices
  - Roast Remix Mode
  - Chaos Certificate (Red Edition)
  - Export without footer

## Chaos Pass
- Price: $24 (One-time payment)
- Features:
  - Everything in Pro
  - Monday push notifications
  - Chaos Certificate (Gold Edition)
  - First access to future voices
  - Support the developer directly
