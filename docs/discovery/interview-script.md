# ScrollShame: Customer Discovery Interview Script

**Goal:** Understand the "Job to be Done" (JTBD) for ScrollShame. 
**Target:** Users who have shared their report at least twice.

## 1. Intro & Context Setting (5 mins)
"We're trying to figure out why people actually use this chaos machine. No right or wrong answers, just be honest."

## 2. The Trigger (10 mins)
- "Tell me about the last time you opened your Monday Report."
- "What were you doing right before that?"
- "What was the first thing you looked at on the card?"
- "Did you share it? Why / why not?"

## 3. The "Chaos" Pivot (10 mins)
- "The score used to be called 'Shame Score', now it's 'Chaos Score'. Does that change how you feel about a high number?"
- "Do you feel 'proud' of a high Chaos Score, or is it still something you want to hide?"
- "If you got a 'Clean Week' (Score < 20), did that feel like a win or was it boring?"

## 4. Value & Monetization (10 mins)
- "If ScrollShame disappeared tomorrow, what would you miss most?"
- "You bought the [Pro/Chaos Pass]. What was the exact moment you decided 'Okay, I'm paying for this'?"
- "Is there anything you'd pay $50 for in this app?" (The 'Magic Wand' question)

## 5. The Competition (5 mins)
- "How did you track your browsing habits before this? (RescueTime, Freedom, Cold Turkey, etc.)"
- "What does this do that those don't?"

## Red Flags to Watch For (Anti-Patterns)
- **Confirmation Bias:** Don't ask "Do you like the roasts?" Ask "Tell me about a roast that didn't work for you."
- **The Mom Test:** Don't ask "Would you use [Feature X]?" Ask "When was the last time you tried to [Action related to Feature X]?"
