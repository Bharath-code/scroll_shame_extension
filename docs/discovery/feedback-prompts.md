# ScrollShame Feedback Prompts

We need high-signal, low-friction ways to gather data without breaking the "Entertainment" immersion.

## 1. In-App Micro-Surveys (Low Friction)
Place these at the bottom of the Monday Report:

- **Prompt A (Humor check):** 
  "Did this roast actually hit? 🎯 / 💨 (Miss)"
  *Goal: Validate if specific voices/lines are getting stale.*

- **Prompt B (Value check):** 
  "What's missing from your chaos? [ ] More voices [ ] A way to hide the evidence [ ] A global leaderboard"
  *Goal: Prioritize P2 features.*

## 2. Post-Purchase (Monetization Check)
On the "Chaos Pass" success page:

- **Question:** "What was the one thing that made you upgrade?"
  *Options: [ ] I wanted the other voices [ ] To support the chaos [ ] To get the Gold Certificate [ ] To remove the free watermark*

## 3. Churn/Uninstall (Exit Signal)
If possible, redirect to a simple landing page on uninstall:

- **Question:** "Too much chaos?"
  *Options: [ ] It got annoying [ ] It wasn't roasting me enough [ ] I fixed my habits (good luck) [ ] Just cleaning up extensions*

## 4. Social Listening Prompts
Questions to ask on X (@ScrollShame):
- "Who's your favorite roaster and why is it Your Mom?"
- "Drop your Chaos Score below. Highest score gets a free Chaos Pass."
- "What's the most 'Grand Chaos' thing you've done in a browser this week?"
