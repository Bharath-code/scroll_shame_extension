# ScrollShame Discovery Board

| Area | What we know | What we're guessing (Assumptions) | Risk Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Monetization** | Subscriptions for novelty apps have high churn. | One-time "Chaos Pass" ($24) will have 2x conversion of $4.99/mo. | 🔴 HIGH |
| **Branding** | "Shame" was punitive and felt judgmental. | "Chaos" is aspirational and acts as a "badge of honor." | 🔴 HIGH |
| **Virality** | Users share "wrapped" style receipts. | Users will post "Chaos Certificates" to professional networks (LinkedIn). | 🟡 MEDIUM |
| **Retention** | Streaks work for Duolingo/fitness apps. | A "Weekly Chaos Streak" is enough to prevent extension uninstalls. | 🟡 MEDIUM |

## Target Learning Goals (Next 2 Weeks)
1. **The "Why" of Sharing:** Do users share because the score is high (chaos) or because the roast was funny?
2. **The Pricing Ceiling:** Is $24 too high for an impulse buy? Should we anchor it against a $49 "Life Chaos" tier?
3. **The Utility Gap:** Do users actually want a "Mute Chaos" button for 2 hours, or does that break the brand?

## Discovery Anti-Patterns to Avoid
- **Skipping to Solutions:** Don't build the "Chaos Replay" video feature until we know people care about the static reports.
- **Only Asking Power Users:** We need feedback from people who install and uninstall within 24 hours.
