## Full Description

### Every week, your browser is writing a report on you.

You know the feeling. It's 1:47am. You have 38 tabs open. You've opened and closed the same Reddit thread four times. You're not sure what you're looking for. Your browser, however, has been keeping notes.

**ScrollShame** turns those notes into a weekly Chaos Report — a receipted summary of your browser behaviour, delivered every Monday, narrated by a voice you didn't ask for.

---

### What you get

**📊 Your Weekly Chaos Score**
A number between 0 and 100 representing the structural integrity of your browsing week. 0 is suspicious. 100 is a cry for help.

**📋 The Receipt**
Your week, line by line:
- Max Simultaneous Crimes (peak tabs open)
- Late Night Incidents (sessions after midnight)
- Tabs You Ghosted (opened and closed in under 60 seconds)
- Full Sends (4+ hour browser marathons)
- Haunted Tabs (scroll velocity events — you know the ones)

**🎙️ Roast Voice — 5 Narrators**
Choose your narrator. They all have opinions about your behaviour.
- **The Therapist** — concerned, gentle, increasingly worried
- **Drill Sergeant** — zero tolerance for your nonsense
- **Your Mum** — disappointed, but still loves you
- **Tech Bro** — concerned this will affect your productivity metrics
- **The Accountant** — has calculated the cost of your decisions

**📈 Score Delta — Week Over Week**
Was this week worse? Suspiciously better? You'll know. And you'll share it.

**🔥 Chaos Streak**
Consecutive weeks tracked. Week 8 of Chaos. Therapy would be cheaper.

**🏆 Chaos Certificate**
Score 80+? Download an official diploma. Share it. You earned it.

**🔁 Roast Remix Mode**
All 5 voices. Same week. One panel. The full committee weighs in. *(Pro)*

**Your data never leaves your browser.** Everything is stored in `chrome.storage.local`. No account. No server. No one watching. Except your browser. Which has been watching the whole time.
